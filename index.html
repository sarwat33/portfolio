Mock Paper in IEEE Conference Format for Course: EEE222 (Circuit Simulation and Shop Practise) 2026
Adjustable DC to DC Boost Converter using NE555 Timer IC
Sarwat Razzaque Choudhury, Ihtesamul Haque Samir,MD.Naimur Rahman Toky
This paper presents the design and experimental validation of an adjustable DC–DC boost converter built around the NE555 timer IC in astable mode. The proposed topology achieves a 8.88x boosting with a 9 V input while delivering up to 80 V output, making it suitable for portable and battery operated systems. Switching frequency optimisation and MOSFET gate drive analysis are discussed in detail.
